The town

Dutch fans descend on the area on race day and each year a dense crowd wearing orange line the streets around Notre Dame des Neiges Church. The Alpe d’Heuz office de tourisme was originally cautious on paying to host the stage but now does so willingly, citing around €10m of annual economic boost.
