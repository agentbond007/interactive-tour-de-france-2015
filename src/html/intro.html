The climb of
Alpe d'Huez

For the 29th time the Tour de France will feature its most iconic Alpine ascent when the peloton arrives at the base of Alpe d’Huez for the climax of stage 20. We take a closer look at what it takes to tackle the 14km climb to the summit, and some of the Tour’s most memorable moments.
